Root Deletion Method

This visualization uses the inorder successor approach when deleting the root node with two children.

The inorder successor is the minimum value in the right subtree (leftmost node of the right child).

Note: The inorder predecessor (maximum value in the left subtree) also works and is equally valid. Both methods maintain the BST property.
